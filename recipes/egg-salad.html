Egg salad.

Description.

You don't know what to eat your bread with, let me introduce you to the egg salad. As the name suggests... well it is just that: an egg salad. So let's get to the ingredients.

Ingredients.

  • Eggs.
  • Mustard.
  • Mayonnaise.
  • Green onion.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper
  • Paprika.

Steps.

  1. Boil, peel, and chop the eggs.
  2. Combine the chopped eggs and the remaining ingredients.
  3. Serve on bread or whatever you want.

Moro de guandules con coco.

Description.

Moro de guandules is a dominican dish that's consist in rice with pingeon peas. It's a easy dish to do, but that don't change how good it tastes or how good is it as an acccompaniment to any other dish.

Well, now that you have a idea of what is the moro de guandules, let's do it!. You will need:

Ingredients.

  • 2 pound of rice.
  • 1 sopita. (Chiken bouillon cube, if you dont find one you can use chiken stock)
  • 1/4 of onion.
  • 1/4 cubanela pepper.
  • Oil. (can be whatever you want)
  • Concentrated tomato paste.
  • 2 garlic cloves.
  • Cilantro.
  • 1 can of pingeon peas.
  • 1 can of coconut milk.

Steps

  1. In a large cauldron you gonna put a little bit of oil and put it to heat at medium heat.
  2. When the oil is hot you gona put the 1/4 of onion, the 1/4 of cubanela peper, 1/2 spoon of concentrated tomato paste, the 2 garlic cloves (sliced), a bit of cilantro (sliced) and the sopita and let it fry for like 3-4 minutes.
  3. While everything is cooking in te cauldron, go and wash the rice 2 times.
  4. Now you add the can of coconut milk and the can of pingeons peas. let it mix the flavor for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the rice and mix it.
  6. Now we cover the cauldron and let it boil. Stir from time to time.
  7. When we see that there is not more water in the bottom of the pot and the rice is cooked (taste to corroborate this) you will know that you successfully made a moro of guandules.

Now you just have to serve the dish and enjoy!.
